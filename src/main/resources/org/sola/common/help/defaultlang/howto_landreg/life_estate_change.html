Change Life Estate

If you need to change the details of a registered Life Estate, use the Change Right or Restriction service. This service allows you to change the details for any right or restriction.

Steps

    1.  Start the Change Right or Restriction service. The Property Details screen will open.
    2. On the Rights / Restrictions tab, select the Life Estate right you intend to change. If the Life Estate right is current, theVary tool will enable. ClickVary to open the Life Estate screen. 
    3. Update the life estate details as appropriate and set the Memorial text. If the life estate was migrated from LRS, you may need to populate the right holder and document details before you can save your changes.
    4. ClickCreate & Save and close the Life Estate screen.
    5. Varying a right creates a new pending version of that right. If you need to make further changes to the life estate information, select the pending Life Estate in the Rights / Restrictions tab and click theEdit tool.
    6. If you need to remove the pending Life Estate details completely, use theRemove tool on the Rights / Restrictions tab.
    7. Save changes to Property Details andComplete the service.
