Record a Lands and Titles Court Judgment

Lands and Titles Court Judgments can be scanned and uploaded into SOLA for easy reference by all staff.

Steps

    1. From the Document Search screen, clickAdd.
    2. In the New Document screen, set the Type to Lands and Titles Court Judgment and enter the other document details as appropriate
    3. Use the Attachment field to link a scanned copy of the judgment to the new document record.
    4. ClickSave & Close.
