Changing your SOLA Password

During your first login to SOLA you will be prompted to change your password. Enter your current and new password and click Save new password. Note that passwords must be at least 5 characters.

You can change your password at any time by using the User profile feature. SOLA will also prompt you to change your password every 90 days.

If you forget your password or your account becomes locked out, you will need to ask the Public Access Computer Administrator to reset your password for you.
