Cancel Life Estate

To cancel a life estate over a property, use the Cancel Life Estate service.

Steps

    1. Start the Cancel Life Estate service. The Property Details screen will open.
    2. On the Rights / Restrictions tab, select the Life Estate right you intend to cancel. If the Life Estate right is current and there are no pending changes for the Life Estate , theExtinguish tool will enable. Click Extinguish to open the Life Estate screen. 
    3. Update the life estate details as appropriate and set the Memorial text. If the life estate was migrated from LRS, you may need to populate the right holder and document details before you can save your changes.
    4. ClickCreate & Save and close the Life Estate screen.
    5. Canceling a right creates a new pending version of that right. If you need to make further changes to the life estate information, select the pending Life Estate in the Rights / Restrictions tab and click theEdit tool.
    6. If you need to reverse the cancellation, use theRemove tool on the Rights / Restrictions tab or remove the pending right.
    7. Save changes to Property Details andComplete the service
