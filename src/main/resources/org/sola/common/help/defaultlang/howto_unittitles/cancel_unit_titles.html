Cancel a Unit Title Development

A Unit Title Development may be cancelled due to two main reasons

    • An application by the registered properties
    • An application to the Court

To cancel a Unit Title Development, you need to use the Cancel Unit Plan service.

Steps

    1. Lodge an application with the Cancel Unit Plan service. You must reference a folio from the Unit Title Development on the Property tab. The recommended folio to use is the Common Property folio (e.g. CP/11000).
    2. Ensure you are Assignee for the application with the Cancel Unit Plan service and open it from your Dashboard.
    3. On the Services tab,Start the Cancel Unit Plan service. This will open the Unit Title screen.
    4. Go to the Strata Properties tab and click theTerminate tool. This will flag all of the unit title folios for cancellation. You will be able to confirm the cancellation has been set successfully as theTerminate tool will be replaced with theCancel termination tool. TheCancel termination tool can be used to undo the cancellation up until approval of the application.
    5. Close the Unit Titles screen.
    6. Complete the Cancel Unit Plan service. If any business rule failures occur, verify the cause of the failures and fix the errors.
    7. Continue processing the application and use theApprove tool to cancel all of the unit title folios.

Notes

    • At approval of the Cancel Unit Plan application, all of the unit title folios will be made historic including the Common Property folio. If the underlying property is a Freehold estate, it will be re-instated and all unit owners from the Unit Title Development will gain a share in the underlying property. The shares allocated to each unit title owner will be based on their unit entitlement and the share structure from their unit title folio. Important: If any unit title folio has a complex ownership structure, you should verify the share allocations calculated by SOLA as there can be minor inaccuracies due to mathematical rounding.  Where inaccuracies are detected, use a Correct Registry service to fix the allocations. 
    • If the underlying property is Leasehold, the unit title folios will be cancelled however the Leasehold property will remain unchanged.
