Survey Services

Survey Services can be used to capture details from new plans and update the cadastral network information recorded in SOLA.

 

Service Name

Description

Documents Reqd.

Record Plan

 

Used to capture the details from new plans (i.e. Survey, Complied, Registry, etc) that require changes to the cadastral network such as the creation of new parcels by subdivision. This service also allows the creation or modification of roads and hydro parcels (e.g. streams, rivers, lakes, sea, etc) as defined by the survey. A fee is charged for this service based on the number of new lots created by the plan.

Plan

Record Plan with No Coordinates

Used to allocation a plan number to a survey with no surveyed coordinates. Commonly used to illustrate indicative boundaries for customary land holdings.

Plan

Record Unit Plan

Used to capture the unit parcels (principal units and accessory units) from a new Unit Title Development.

Unit Plan

Change Map

Used to make changes to the cadastral network outside of the receipt of a plan. Examples of map changes include updating official road names, creating new hydro parcels (e.g. streams, rivers, lakes, sea, etc) and/or correcting the topology of existing parcel boundaries. No fee is changed for this service.
