Add Ownership Details to a Property

You can add ownership details to a property using either the Register New Ownership service or the New Digital Title service. Register New Ownership applies if the property exists in SOLA and the ownership details are changing due to sale of the property or a related ownership transfer transaction. The New Digital Title service can be used to create new property records in SOLA as well as register all of the necessary rights / restrictions to the property including ownership, leases, restrictions, etc

Using an assigned application, start the ownership related service from the Application Details Services tab to open the Property Details screen. On the Rights / Restrictions tab select the Ownership right (this may be pre-selected for the Register New Ownership service) and click Create. This will open the Ownership details screen.



Tick the Primary Right checkbox to indicate this is the primary right for the property. Enter a suitable Notation and add references to documents that justify the ownership change.

Ownership rights consist of one or more shares which are expressed as fractions. The fractions from all current shares on the property must sum to 1. To enter a new share for the property, click Add in the Shares section

A share may be allocated to one or more parties. A party can be an individual or an entity. Shares that are allocated to more than one individual or more than one entity are considered undivided (a.k.a. commonly owned). This means SOLA does not record the exact share for each party, simply that multiple parties participate in the same share. The picture below illustrates an undivided share between John and Jane Smith. In this case the share is 1/1 meaning John and Jane own the entire property between them, however the percentage owned by John vs the percentage owned by Jane is not specified



To add parties to the share, click Add. This will open the Party Details screen allowing you to enter in details for an individual or an entity. Once the necessary party details have been entered, click Create.



Once all parties have been added to the share, click Create on the Share Details screen and once all shares have been added to the Ownership right, click Create on the Ownership Details screen. The Property Details screen will then show a pending Ownership right. Click Save on the Property Details screen to save the new ownership details.



To complete the change in ownership, complete the ownership related service using the Complete tool on the Application Details Services tab and then approve the application.
